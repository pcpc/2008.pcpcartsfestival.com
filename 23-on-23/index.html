23 on 23

23 Explorations on the 23rd Psalm

Written and Collected by Dr. Pete Deison

A beautiful full color book highlighting 80 pieces of work from the 2007 PCPC Arts Festival is now on sale!

As you read the words and see the heartfelt expressions of the artists featured in the book, the 23rd Psalm will come alive for you in new and fresh ways. Your heart will delight in the creativity and these expressions will instill deep motivation for you to see the Great Shepherd. He is the One who created us in such a way that we can enjoy the color, texture, skill and imagination in "23 on 23."

Copies can be purchased for $14 each at:

  • Park Cities Presbyterian Church office
  • Art Delivery Day, March 8
  • 2008 PCPC Arts Festival, April 5 - 13
